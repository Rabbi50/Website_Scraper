Course Description
(Course taken from Newskills Academy USA Library)Learning the material in the Introduction to Podcasting course is essential for anybody who is interested in finding out exactly how podcasting works and how you can start your own podcast quickly and without spending lots of money. This course covers everything you need to know about podcasting, including a full explanation of everything you need to begin, the reasons to start and how to overcome your fears when taking the first step.From the gear and software that you will need and the formats to choose, to how to develop content and produce and publish your podcast, this course is the ultimate introductory guide to podcasting!
