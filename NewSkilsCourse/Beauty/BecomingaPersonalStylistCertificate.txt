Course Description
(Course taken from Newskills Academy USA Library)The personal stylist profession can be lucrative and rewarding in terms of experience. However, just like any other industry, it takes hard work to become a successful image consultant. A professional should learn the basics of color theory, fashion history and keep fashion trends and varied styles and looks at their fingertips.Professional personal stylists help clients choose the best hair, clothes and makeup based on their budget, age, profession or type of work and body type. It is important for persons aspiring to take up a career in the industry to learn about what it takes to become a personal stylist or image consultant.This course has been designed to help you learn everything that it takes to become a personal stylist.
