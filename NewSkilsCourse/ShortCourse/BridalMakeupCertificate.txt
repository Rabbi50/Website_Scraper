Course Description
The course covers many topics related to becoming a skilled and effective artist, as well as how to attract a robust clientele. We have developed this course to give students important information about how to succeed in this highly competitive field.Throughout the course, students are provided with instruction, tips and techniques for applying and styling makeup for brides, including methods for priming the face, applying foundation and creating the perfect bridal smoky eye. The course also goes into the specific details involved in starting and running a freelance company. Students of this course further learn about the tools and equipment they need to succeed as a bridal makeup artist.At the conclusion of the course, students receive a diploma, proving they have successfully completed each module.This course was designed to give a total overview of what is involved in becoming a freelance bridal makeup artist:Here’s what the course covers:This bridal makeup course includes the following benefits:


Information about the various products and brands that are recommended
